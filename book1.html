Time blocking harness your energy and center it on your most important work . so, go and get a calendar and block off all the time you need to accomplish your ONE thing. #1 BLOCK YOUR TIME OFF

Extraordinary successful people launch their year by taking time out to plan their time off. Why? Because they know they'll need it and they can afford it. In truth,successful people simply see themself working in their vacations. On the other hand least successful people don't plan their time off because they think that they don't deserve it or not be able to afford it. By planning your time off or time to relax you are making clear in your mind which time is dedicated for your work and which time is for your rest. As a result you are more effective in your working time. By planning your time off in advance you're also letting everyone in advance that when you will be free and they can plan accordingly. Remember resting is as important as working.
